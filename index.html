The Alphabet

I for one welcome our new Alphabet overlords

After the announcement that Google is now one of many corporations housed under the wing of the newly-created Alphabet corporation, I reached out to some of my friends to help compile a useful list of possible company names to fill out this new alphabet. After all, you can't really have an alphabet with only one letter (technically you can, but it would make for a very boring language ~~ ed.)

We hope this list will be useful to Page and Brin in their quest to make cool things!

  • Aperture Science. Also Abstergo Industries or Alchemax
  • Buy n Large. Blue Sun is also a fine name, or even BiffCo Enterprises
  • Cyberdyne Systems. Maybe Cobra or Cloverleaf Industries?
  • Dharma Initiative
  • EvilCorp
  • Fontaine Futuristics
  • Google
  • Hyperion Corp.
  • InGen. Can never go wrong with IniTech, if you're going for that business-y feel.
  • J Corp
  • KAOS
  • LexCorp. Los Pollos Hermanos if you prefer something more brotherly.
  • M.A.D.. A bunch of great options for M: M.A.R.S. Industries, MomCorp, or Multi-National United, all of which really encompass future and unity.
  • New Path
  • OCP
  • Primatech
  • Quantum
  • Roxxon. Rekall is a great option too, more tech-oriented.
  • SPECTRE. Soylent would have been the preferred choice, but it's already taken. Shinra could appeal to the Asian markets.
  • Tyrell Corporation. We hope to see some good cybernetic organisms out of T!
  • Umbrella Corp.
  • Value-Tec. Versalife or Virtucon would also work here.
  • Weyland-Yutani
  • Xanatos Enterprises
  • Yoyodyne Propulsion Systems
  • Zorg Industries
